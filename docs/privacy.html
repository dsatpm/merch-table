Merch Table — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Merch Table is an offline merch-table sales tracker. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data collection

Merch Table does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no backend server, no user accounts, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Data storage

All data you enter — items, prices, sales, inventory counts — is stored locally on your device only, using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself (for example, via the app's CSV export or share feature).

Permissions

The Android app requests internet access for planned future functionality (such as sharing summaries). No data is currently sent over the network by the app.

Third parties

Merch Table does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-processing services.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised via the project's GitHub repository: github.com/dsatpm/merch-table.
